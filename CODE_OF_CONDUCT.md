# Code of Conduct

> Sheetifye is built in public, for everyone. We hold ourselves to a high standard — and we expect the same from everyone who participates.

---

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in Sheetifye and its community a **harassment-free experience for everyone** — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ We encourage

- Using welcoming, inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and lived experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritising what is best for the community as a whole
- Showing genuine empathy toward other community members

### ❌ We do not tolerate

- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Sharing others' private information — physical or digital — without explicit consent
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable standards of behaviour and are expected to take fair, consistent corrective action in response to any violations.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct — and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor whose behaviour is deemed inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public space where an individual represents Sheetifye or its community (e.g. using an official project email, posting from an official account, or acting as a designated representative at an event).

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by [opening a private issue](https://github.com/vikaspoute/sheetifye/issues) or contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub.

All reports will be:

- Reviewed promptly and investigated thoroughly
- Handled with full confidentiality toward the reporter
- Met with a response proportionate to the circumstances

Maintainers who fail to enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
